Website Evaluations

Bad Website

Arngren

Arngren is a website that seels a variery of products. This does not follow the CRAP principles.

  • Contrast: This site lacks clear contrast, with clashing colors and overlapping elements.
  • Repetition: The site has no repetition, with each page looking different from the last. No consitency in fonts, layour, or colors making it hard to navigate
  • Alignment: The site has no alignment, with elements scattered all over the page.
  • Proximity: The site has no proximity, with too much information being crammmed into small spaces. Making it hard to focus on one thing at a time

Good Website

Apple

Apple is a website that sells a variety of products. This follows the CRAP principles.

  • Contrast: This site has clear contrast, with colors that are easy on the eyes and elements that stand out.
  • Repetition: The site has repetition, with each page looking similar to the last. Consitency in fonts, layout, and colors making it easy to navigate
  • Alignment: The site has alignment, with elements that are organized and easy to follow. Strucuted into grids and balanced spacing making it easy to follow.
  • Proximity: The site has proximity, with information that is grouped together and easy to read. Making it easy to focus on one product at a time
